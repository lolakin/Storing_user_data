What is Thai Massage?


Thai massage is a kind of massage with traditional Thai style that involves deep massage and body stretching.
This form of bodywork is usually performed on a padded floor mat, and the client wears comfortable clothes that enable easy body movement. Oils is not used in Thai massage.

Traditional Thai Massage is relatively new to modern spas here in Nigeria, but actually, it was developed 2,500 years ago by Buddhist monks in Thailand as a healing modality. Thai massage utilizes gentle pressure along the body's energy lines and passive stretching, to increase flexibility, relieve muscle and joint tension and balance the body's energy systems. The process is both deeply energizing and relaxing.
