How Does Couple Massage Work?


A couple massage generates a wonderfully-relaxing, personal experience. During a massage session, two persons, often romantic partners, receive massages simultaneously in the same room from two different therapists. Couple massage therapy has lots of the same benefits as other types of massage, but with added bonuses which will be emphasized below.


Bonding Opportunities


Couple Massage Home Service is a special, shared experience that encourages you and your partner to bond closer together at the comfort of your home or hotel room.
Most of our clients often utilise the couple massage session to catch up, communicate and chat, while others simply relax in comfortable silence and bask in their shared, romantic experience.
Even when words are not spoken during a session, couple massage brings couples closer to each other.


Increased Comfort


Unlike other types of massage that are performed solo, couple massage treatments allow our clients to experience therapy with someone they know well.
This familiarity brings enormous comfort to first time clients, who may initially be uncomfortable or nervous about the process.
Having a partner in the room makes the session less intimidating and allows our clients to relax more fully.
Many clients who are initially uneasy about undressing are more comfortable doing so in the presence of a dear friend or partner.


Convenient Co-ordination


Couples with busy schedules find it difficult to find time for each other, let alone massages.
By our innovative pairing of massage sessions with quality bonding time, couples can achieve two goals at the same time, and emerge relaxed, refreshed and reconnected with each other.
The convenience that couple massage treatments brings to the table is also ideal for partners who are planning a special event, like a wedding or anniversary.
Entire bridal parties, for example, can use our couple massage therapy to get ready for a wedding all at once, thereby reducing rushing and stress.


Personalized Massage Therapy Together


Every person has his/her personal needs and goals for massage treatment. During a session for a couple, each client can receive unique treatments that address his/her specific needs.
In certain scenarios, while one partner may experience pain emanating from the shoulders or neck, the other partner may experience tightness in the feet or legs.
Couple massage sessions allow any of these issues to be treated at once, resulting in comprehensive and complete therapy for both partners.


A Healing, Healthy Experience


Research proves that massage can reduce stress and muscle tension, enhance blood circulation, eliminate stiffness and soreness, boost the immune system, reduce pain and even help control one's blood pressure.
Couples who practice a healthy lifestyle can enjoy these benefits that come with couple massage together.
